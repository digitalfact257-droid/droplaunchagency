FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with DropLaunch Agency.

How long does setup take?
Depending on the service, most projects are launched within 7–21 days. A basic dropshipping store takes 7 days, while a full brand + funnel system typically takes 14–21 days. We move fast without compromising quality.
Do you work internationally?
Absolutely. We currently work with clients across 15+ countries including USA, UK, UAE, Pakistan, India, Germany, Australia and more. All communication is done remotely via WhatsApp, Zoom and email.
What platforms do you use?
We work with Shopify, WooCommerce, WordPress, Webflow, ClickFunnels, GoHighLevel, Kajabi, Teachable, Gumroad, Klaviyo, DSers, AutoDS, and many more. We choose the best tools for your specific business needs.
Do you provide ongoing support?
Yes. Every project includes a dedicated support period after launch. We also offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing strategy, management and scaling support so your business keeps growing.
Can complete beginners get started?
Yes — most of our clients start with zero experience. We handle everything for you and provide full training so you understand your business. No technical knowledge required. If you're motivated to succeed, we'll build the rest.
